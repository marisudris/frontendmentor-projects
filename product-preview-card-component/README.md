### What I learned

Margins within grid tracks sized with `auto` work different from what you'd expect in block layout.
Putting margins on grid items doesn't just push away its neighbors, they also affect the size of the grid track and item within its cell.

### Continued development

Resarch grid layout and its relationship to margins more thoroughly.
